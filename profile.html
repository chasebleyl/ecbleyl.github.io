About Positive Learning

Positive Learning's goal is to help teachers and students in standards competency and understanding of the material in those standards. With focused reporting and assessments, teachers have the ability to formatively assess how their students are performing, and with a robust resource bank students are able to solidy the learning process. Their responsive application allows this process to happen anywhere a student needs to learn.

About Second Story Capital

Started by the former founder of ParentLink, Second Story Capital takes its experience in the Ed-Tech industry and looks to help aid companies in "improving student achievement." With partners having experience in many different fields, from energy and finance to education and marketing, the Second Story Capital team looks to guide smart entrepreneurs width innovate ideas.
